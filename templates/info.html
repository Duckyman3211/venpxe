The VenPXE Project

Open-Source multi-iso network boot menu with diagnostic tools.

Why

VenPXE was made as a alternative to iVentoy and NetbootXYZ. That has features of both while being diffrent and easy to install.

It also is being made to run on low performace hardware. As long as it has a wifi or lan adapter it works.

VenPXE is free and open-Source software released under the GNU GPL 3.0 License.
