Core Vision Statement

VenPXE was conceptualized out of frustration with clunky, corporate network provisioning platforms that impose hidden subscription gates, tracking frameworks, and require heavy enterprise relational database footprints.

By compiling dynamic configuration routing structures straight out of memory directly down through the low-level iPXE script interface layer, VenPXE allows thousands of complex data systems to configure them silently, efficiently, and with zero configuration assumptions.

VenPXE is free software released under the transparent terms of the MIT License model. Modify, break, distribute, and enhance it endlessly.
